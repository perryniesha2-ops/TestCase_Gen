Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-20

This Privacy Policy explains how SynthQA (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our AI-powered test case generator and related services (the “Service”).

What we collect

  • Account & identity data: name, email address, (optional) company name.
  • App content: requirements you enter, generated test cases, test cases you add or edit, descriptions, titles, and related metadata.
  • Usage & device data: events (e.g., button clicks, errors), device/browser info, and diagnostic logs.
  • Network data: IP address (planned future collection), timestamps, and request metadata.

How we use your information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service.
  • Generate, store, and let you manage test cases (including cross-platform test cases).
  • Authenticate users, secure accounts, and prevent abuse.
  • Measure usage, debug issues, and develop new features.
  • Communicate with you (service updates, security alerts, and support).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

AI model providers & subprocessors

We use third-party processors to deliver the Service, including:

  • OpenAI — large language models and embeddings.
  • Anthropic — large language models.
  • Database/hosting (e.g., Supabase/Postgres or equivalent) — storage and authentication.
  • Operational tools (logging, analytics, email) as needed.

Where available, we opt out of provider model-training on your content.

Legal bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract — to provide the Service.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure, improve, and analyze.
  • Consent — where required (e.g., certain cookies/marketing).
  • Legal obligation — compliance with law.

Data retention

We retain data while your account is active and as needed to provide the Service. You can request deletion; limited records may be kept for legal, security, or billing purposes.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect information in transit and at rest, but no method is 100% secure.

International transfers

Your information may be processed in the United States and other countries where our vendors operate. Appropriate safeguards are applied where required.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data, or object to certain processing. Contact: support@sythnqa.app.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction).

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are material, we’ll provide notice (e.g., in-app or by email).

Contact

Questions or requests: support@synthqa.app.
